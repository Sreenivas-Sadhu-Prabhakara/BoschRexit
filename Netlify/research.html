Research Methodology

Uncovering the "Why" behind customer behavior.

Phase 1: Exploratory (The "Why")

120 In-depth Interviews
15 Focus Groups
10 Ethnographic Observations

Discovery: The "Overburdened Integrator"

The Insight: We assumed customers wanted "Advanced AI". We were wrong.

Qualitative interviews revealed that engineers were overwhelmed. They didn't have time to learn complex AI systems. Their #1 need was not intelligence, but speed of implementation.

"I don't need a cylinder that thinks. I need a cylinder that installs itself." — Senior Controls Engineer, Food & Bev Packaging

Phase 2: Quantitative (The "What")

Metric Value
Sample Size 1,000
Response Rate 25%
Margin of Error ±3%

Strategic Checkpoint

This phase prevented engineering bias toward advanced technology. It proved that usability was the killer feature.
