Bosch Rexroth Case Study

How marketing research redefined automation from "Tech Push" to "Integration Speed"

The Bottom Line

Top Message

Bosch Rexroth used marketing research not to validate an idea — but to redesign automation around integration speed.

This shifted Smart MechatroniX into a “Plug-and-Produce” platform that reduced customer risk and accelerated adoption faster than competitors.

The Core Transformation

Old Focus

Advanced AI Features

New Strategy

Ease of Integration

3:1 Preference for Integration Ease vs AI Features
30-50% Reduction in Customer Setup Time
Risk Shifted from Technology to Operations

Strategic Logic

  • Observation: Industry frustration was driven by integration complexity, not a lack of AI features.
  • Reframing: Research shifted value proposition from Innovation Innovation to Operational Speed.
  • Execution: Engineering architecture and launch messaging prioritized "Plug-and-Produce" simplicity.
