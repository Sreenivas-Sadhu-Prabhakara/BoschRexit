✔ What Worked

  • Simplicity Selling: Engineers preferred the solution that made their daily lives easier over the one with the most theoretical features.
  • Ecosystem Effect: Open interfaces (RestAPI, OPC UA) encouraged third-party adoption, creating a moat.
  • Brand Modernization: Successfully re-positioned Bosch Rexroth from a "Hydraulics Giant" to a "Factory of the Future Partner."

⚠️ Lessons Learned

Challenge Insight
Sales Channel Inertia Traditional sales teams needed retraining to sell "software value" vs "hardware specs."
Pricing Models Subscription-based features faced initial resistance in capex-heavy industries.

Final Summary

The Smart MechatroniX project proved that in mature industrial markets, integration experience is the new innovation.

By listening to the "hidden" customer pains, Bosch Rexroth didn't just launch a product; they launched a new standard for industrial automation.
